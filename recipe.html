Air Fried Kale Chips

Description

A tasty crisp delight that can satify anyones diet.

Ingredients

  • Two cups of Kale
  • Olive oil
  • Sea salt

Directions

  1. Wash and conpletely dry Kale.
  2. Apply olive oil to kale via spray of baste.
  3. Sprinkle black pepper and salt on top.
  4. Preheat your air fryer at 360 degrees, five minutes.
  5. Put kale in air fryer for 10 minutes or less.
